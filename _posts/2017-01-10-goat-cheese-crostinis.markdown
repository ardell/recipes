Ingredients
-----------
- 1 loaf French bread
- 1 log goat cheese
- honey
- pepper

Instructions
------------
Slice French bread on the bias. Spread out on a cookie sheet and toast in the
oven.

On each slice, spread a small spoonful of goat cheese. Drizzle with honey and
sprinkle with fresh cracked pepper.
